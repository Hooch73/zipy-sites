Our Story

Lloyds Cafe is a cosy cafe in the heart of Howick, founded by Lloyd Brown. We are passionate about creating a warm, welcoming space for the community to gather, relax, and enjoy high-quality food and beverages.

We pride ourselves on serving exceptional coffees made by our award-winning barista, along with a delightful selection of teas, smoothies, delicious pastries, and simple healthy meals. Every cup and every plate is prepared with love and the finest local ingredients.
