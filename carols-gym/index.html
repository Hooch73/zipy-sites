Welcome to Carols Gym

Owned and operated by Tom, Carols Gym is a boutique fitness studio in Howick, Auckland, catering specifically to the needs of our retired community. We believe that age is just a number, and maintaining physical health is the key to a happy, independent life.

Our focus is on creating a friendly, supportive, and non-intimidating environment where you can comfortably work on retaining your strength and improving your balance. We're here to help you enjoy your golden years to the fullest, with confidence and vitality.
