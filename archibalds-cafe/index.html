Our Story

Welcome to Archibalds Cafe, a sanctuary for coffee connoisseurs and dessert lovers in Howick, Auckland. We are dedicated to bringing you an unparalleled cafe experience, focusing on the vibrant and complex flavors of authentic African coffee beans, ethically sourced and expertly roasted. Our passion extends to our artisanal desserts, each a unique creation designed to complement the richness of our coffees.

Founded with a vision to share the authentic taste of Africa, Archibalds Cafe is more than just a place to grab a coffee; it's a destination where culture, community, and exceptional culinary art converge. Come, discover your new favorite indulgence.
