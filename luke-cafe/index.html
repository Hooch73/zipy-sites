About Us

Nestled in the heart of Howick, Auckland, Luke Cafe is a cozy retreat dedicated to the rich traditions of American coffee and homemade desserts. We believe in simple pleasures: a perfectly brewed cup of coffee, a slice of decadent pie, and a warm, welcoming space to enjoy them. Our passion is to bring a taste of classic American comfort to our community, using quality ingredients and a lot of love. Come in, relax, and make yourself at home.
