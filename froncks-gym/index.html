About Froncks Gym

Welcome to Froncks Gym, a unique boutique fitness studio in the heart of Howick, Auckland, founded by Tom. We are passionately dedicated to the health and well-being of our retired community. Our mission is simple: to help you retain essential strength and balance, ensuring a healthy, happy, and fulfilling lifestyle for years to come.

We understand the specific needs that come with age, and our programs are carefully designed to improve physical quality of life, boost confidence, and maintain independence. Join our supportive community and take a proactive step towards living your fullest life.

Our Services

We offer specialized programs to help retired people maintain their physical quality of life and live fully.

Strength & Balance Classes

Join our engaging group classes designed to improve core strength, stability, and coordination in a fun, social environment. Perfect for building confidence and making new friends.

One-on-One Sessions

Receive personalized attention with our private training sessions. We'll create a custom fitness plan tailored to your specific goals, abilities, and health considerations.

Lifestyle Coaching

Our holistic approach includes guidance on maintaining a healthy, active lifestyle outside the gym, helping you integrate wellness into your daily routine for lasting benefits.
