A Taste of Africa in the Heart of Howick

At Mathews Cafe, we don't just serve coffee; we curate an experience. Founded by Lloyd Brown, our mission is to bring the distinct, earthy, and aromatic profiles of African coffee beans to New Zealand.

Whether you are craving a robust Ethiopian blend or a delicate dessert to pair with your afternoon brew, our space on Elliot Street is designed for comfort, conversation, and culinary delight.
