About Us

Welcome to Franks Deli, your new favorite spot in South Yarra for both you and your furry friend! We're passionate about providing high-quality, delicious food for everyone, including your canine companions. We believe that dogs deserve the best, which is why we offer a wide range of raw, frozen, and dried dog treats made with only the finest ingredients. At Franks Deli, you'll find not only delectable treats for your pup but also a selection of gourmet deli items for yourself. From artisanal cheeses and cured meats to freshly baked breads and savory sandwiches, we have something to satisfy every craving. Our friendly staff is always happy to help you find the perfect treat or meal, and we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming and enjoyable experience for all our customers. Come visit us today and discover the perfect treats for you and your four-legged pal! We can't wait to welcome you to the Franks Deli family.
