About Us

Welcome to Franks Deli, Melbourne's premier destination for discerning dog owners! We believe your furry friend deserves the very best, which is why we've curated a selection of boutique, organic dog food and treats sourced from only the finest producers around the globe. From delectable wet food to convenient kibble and refreshing frozen options, we cater to every pup's palate and dietary needs. At Franks Deli, quality is our top priority. We meticulously research and select each product to ensure it meets our high standards for nutrition, taste, and ethical sourcing. Our knowledgeable team is passionate about dogs and dedicated to helping you find the perfect food to keep your beloved companion happy, healthy, and thriving. Come visit us and discover a world of delicious and nutritious possibilities for your four-legged family member!
