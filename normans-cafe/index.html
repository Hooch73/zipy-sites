Welcome to Normans

Crafted with Passion, Served with a Smile

Nestled in the heart of Howick, Auckland, Normans Cafe is your go-to destination for an authentic taste of American coffee culture and heavenly desserts. We pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients and pouring our hearts into every cup and creation.

Founded by Lloyd Brown, Normans Cafe is more than just a business; it's a passion project dedicated to bringing joy through delicious food and beverages. Come experience the warmth and flavour that makes us a local favourite.
