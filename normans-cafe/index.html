Discover Normans Cafe

Welcome to Normans Cafe, your cozy corner in Howick, Auckland, where the aroma of freshly brewed American coffee blends perfectly with the sweet allure of our signature desserts. We pride ourselves on creating a warm, inviting atmosphere for our community.

Founded by Lloyd Brown, Normans Cafe is more than just a coffee shop; it's a destination for moments of joy and delicious indulgence. We believe in quality ingredients, exceptional service, and a taste of genuine American sweetness.
