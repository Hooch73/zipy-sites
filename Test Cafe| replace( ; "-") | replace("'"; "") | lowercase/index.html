Our Story

Welcome to Test Cafe, a culinary haven nestled in Howick, Auckland. We are passionate about bringing the refined artistry of Japanese coffee brewing and the delicate elegance of Japanese desserts to our beloved community. From the rich, nuanced flavors of our meticulously prepared coffee to the delightful sweetness of our handcrafted pastries, every item is a testament to quality and tradition.

Founded by Lloyd Brown, Test Cafe is more than just a place to eat and drink; it's an experience. We invite you to unwind, connect, and indulge in the serene atmosphere that blends modern comfort with a touch of Japanese tranquility.

What We Offer

Authentic Japanese Coffee

Experience the true essence of Japanese coffee culture with our expertly brewed single-origin beans and traditional methods like siphon and pour-over.

Artisanal Desserts

Indulge in our exquisite range of Japanese-inspired desserts, from delicate mochi and matcha parfaits to seasonal fruit tarts.

Freshly Made Pastries

Enjoy a selection of freshly baked pastries, perfect for pairing with your coffee, offering a delightful blend of textures and flavors.

Serene Ambiance

Relax and rejuvenate in our calming cafe environment, designed to offer a peaceful escape from the everyday hustle.
