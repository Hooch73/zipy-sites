Your Partner in Healthy Aging

Frank Gym is a unique, boutique fitness space in Howick, Auckland, founded by Tom. We are dedicated to the health and well-being of the retired community. Our mission is to provide a supportive, welcoming environment where you can focus on retaining essential strength and balance. We believe that an active lifestyle is key to a happy and independent retirement, and our tailored programs are designed to help you maintain your physical quality of life to live every day to the fullest.

Our Services

One-on-One Sessions

Receive personalized attention with training plans tailored specifically to your individual goals, needs, and fitness level.

Group Exercise Classes

Join our friendly and motivating strength and balance classes. A great way to stay active, meet new people, and have fun.

Balance & Stability

Specialized exercises designed to improve your stability, build confidence, and reduce the risk of falls in a safe environment.

Lifestyle Maintenance

Our programs focus on maintaining the physical quality of life necessary to enjoy your retirement and live a full, independent life.
