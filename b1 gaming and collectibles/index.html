About Our Shop

Welcome to B1 Gaming And Collectibles, the heart of hobby culture in Howick, Auckland. Founded by passionate collector Lloyd Brown, our shop is a haven for enthusiasts of all ages. We believe that play and collecting are timeless pursuits that connect generations.

We specialise in a carefully curated selection of toys that spark imagination in children and high-quality collectibles that evoke nostalgia in adults. From the latest action figures to rare trading cards, our mission is to provide a friendly, expert, and welcoming space for everyone to share their passion.
