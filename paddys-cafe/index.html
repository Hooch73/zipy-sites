Our Story

Welcome to Paddy's Cafe, your local destination for authentic African coffee. We travel across Africa, from the highlands of Ethiopia to the mountains of Kenya, sourcing the very best single-origin beans. Our mission is to bring you a wide range of unique and delicious coffee flavours, each with a story to tell from the continent where coffee was born.

Meet The Owner

Paddy Baker, a barista with 30 years of experience, is the heart and soul of our cafe. Born in Africa, his passion for coffee is rooted in his heritage. Paddy's expertise and deep knowledge of African coffee ensure that every cup served is a perfect expression of its origin.
