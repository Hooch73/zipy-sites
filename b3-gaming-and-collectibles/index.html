Your Local Hobby Haven

Welcome to B3 Gaming And Collectibles, a passion project brought to life by owner Lloyd Brown. Located in the heart of Howick, we are a friendly, local hobby shop dedicated to serving the community of collectors, gamers, and families. We believe in the joy of play and the thrill of the find, offering a carefully curated selection of items that appeal to both the young and the young-at-heart.

From the latest game consoles to rare vintage toys and sought-after adult collectibles, our mission is to be a place where everyone can share their passion. Come on in, say hello, and discover something amazing!
