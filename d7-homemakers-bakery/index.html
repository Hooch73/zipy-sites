Welcome to Our Bakery Hub

Founded by Ady, D7 HomeMaker's Bakery is a haven for bakers of all levels. We believe that the best creations start with the finest ingredients. Our passion for baking drives us to source and provide a curated selection of high-quality baking supplies, from specialty flours to professional-grade tools.

We are more than just a retail store; we are a community hub in Howick, Auckland, where home bakers and professional pastry chefs alike can find everything they need to bring their delicious visions to life. Come visit us and let us be a part of your baking journey!
