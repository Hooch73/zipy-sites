Our Story

Howick's Home of Comfort Food

Welcome to Liam Restaurant, Howick's heart of classic American dining! Born from a passion for authentic comfort food, our journey began with a simple mission: to bring the nostalgic charm of a true American diner to the vibrant community of Auckland. We believe in quality ingredients, hearty portions, and service with a smile. Every burger is grilled to perfection, every milkshake is spun with care, and every dessert is a sweet slice of happiness. Our cozy spot on Elliot Street is more than just a restaurant; it's a place where families gather, friends reconnect, and great memories are made over even greater food. From our sizzling grill to your plate, we pour our heart into every dish. Come on in, take a seat, and experience a taste of Americana right here in Howick. We can't wait to serve you!
