Welcome to Pauls

A Taste of America in the Heart of Howick

Pauls Restaurant is a family-owned diner dedicated to bringing the classic American dining experience to Auckland. Founded by Lloyd Brown, our passion is simple: serve delicious, comforting food that brings people together.

We specialise in handcrafted burgers, thick milkshakes, and a wide array of decadent desserts, all made with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Whether you're craving a juicy cheeseburger, crispy loaded fries, or a slice of homemade apple pie, we've got something to satisfy your taste buds.

Come on in, take a seat in one of our cozy booths, and let us treat you to a meal you won't forget. We're more than just a restaurant; we're a place for friends and family to make memories.
