A Taste of America

Welcome to Gerald's!

Founded by food enthusiast Lloyd Brown, Gerald Restaurant brings the classic American diner experience to Howick, Auckland. We're passionate about serving up delicious, hearty food that feels both nostalgic and exciting.

Our mission is simple: to create a friendly, welcoming spot where families and friends can gather to enjoy high-quality comfort food. From juicy burgers to decadent milkshakes and homemade desserts, every dish is prepared with love and the finest local ingredients.

Come on in, take a seat, and let us treat you to a meal you won't forget!
