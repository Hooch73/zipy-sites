A Taste of South Africa

Welcome to Zachary Restaurant, where our passion is bringing the vibrant and delicious flavours of South Africa to you. We specialise in authentic South African food, decadent desserts, and refreshing drinks, all served in a warm and welcoming atmosphere.

Meet The Chef

Lloyd Brown has 30 years of experience as a chef in South African cuisine. He has passionately designed a menu that epitomises a real South African restaurant, bringing cherished family recipes and culinary traditions from his homeland to Howick.
