About Us

Welcome to Bob's Lawnmowing! We're a local, family-owned business dedicated to providing top-quality lawn mowing services to the Howick community in Auckland. We believe a well-maintained lawn not only enhances your property's curb appeal but also provides a space for you to enjoy the outdoors with family and friends. At Bob's Lawnmowing, no job is too big or too small. Whether you have a sprawling estate or a cozy backyard, we'll treat your lawn with the care and attention it deserves. We use professional-grade equipment and techniques to ensure a clean, even cut every time. Our friendly and reliable team is committed to providing exceptional customer service and exceeding your expectations. Contact us today for a free quote and let us help you achieve the perfect lawn! We pride ourselves on punctuality and professionalism. We will communicate with you clearly and transparently throughout the process.

Our Services

Regular Lawn Mowing

Our standard lawn mowing service includes cutting the grass to the optimal height, edging along pathways and driveways, and blowing away any clippings. We offer flexible scheduling options to suit your needs, whether you require weekly, fortnightly, or monthly service. We will tailor the service to your specific lawn needs.

Edge Trimming

Precise edge trimming gives your lawn a clean and manicured look. We'll trim along fences, flowerbeds, and other hard-to-reach areas to create a sharp, defined edge. We use industry standard tools to give a professional edge every time.

Weed Control

We offer targeted weed control solutions to keep your lawn looking its best. Our safe and effective treatments eliminate unwanted weeds without harming your grass. We can advise you on the best course of action to keep those weeds under control.

One-Off Lawn Mowing

Need a one-time lawn mow to get your yard back in shape? We offer a one-off lawn mowing service if you just need your lawns cut once. Great to get your lawns back in shape if you have let them go or you are going on holiday.
