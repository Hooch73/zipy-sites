Our Story

Zachs Cafe was born from a love affair with classic American coffee culture and the sweet comfort of homemade desserts. Nestled in the heart of Howick, Auckland, we aim to be your cozy escape, a place where friendly faces and irresistible aromas greet you every day. From rich, bold brews to melt-in-your-mouth pies and cookies, we pour our heart into every offering, using only the finest ingredients to bring you a truly authentic experience.
